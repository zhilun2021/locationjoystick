locationjoystick

Spoof your GPS location on Android. Use a floating joystick, saved routes, or automatic roaming to move your location anywhere while your other apps run normally.

Uses Android's official mock location API. Enable Developer Options, select locationjoystick as your mock location provider, and every app on the device sees the location you choose.

Screens

Home

Main hub. Start, stop, and navigate to all features.

Map

Live map. Long-press to walk or teleport. Launch routes, favorites, and roaming.

Routes

Create and replay waypoint routes. Straight-line or road-following.

Favorites

Save named locations. Teleport or walk there instantly.

Settings

Speed profiles, GPS realism, widget config, export/import, QR transfer.

Overlays

Floating joystick and widget that stay on top of any app.

Onboarding

First-run setup: permissions and mock location provider.

Quick start

  1. Install the app and open it.
  2. Follow onboarding: grant location permission, overlay permission, then set locationjoystick as your mock location app in Developer Options.
  3. Tap Map, tap the start button, then long-press the map to walk or teleport anywhere.
  4. Use the floating widget or joystick overlay to control your location while other apps are in the foreground.
