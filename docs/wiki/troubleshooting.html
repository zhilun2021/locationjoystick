Troubleshooting

Common setup problems and how to fix them.

App missing from the "Select mock location app" list

Open the app at least once before checking the list — a freshly installed app will not appear until it has been launched. Then turn Developer Options off and back on, or restart your device, so the system refreshes the list.

On some phones (MIUI, ColorOS, and other custom Android skins), the system hides newly installed apps from this list until you grant an additional "allow restricted settings" permission from the app's info page, or until after a reboot. If the app still does not appear, try reinstalling it, then reboot once more before checking again.

Mock location not detected

Some devices require rebooting after changing the Developer Options setting. Try rebooting, then tap Check again.

Overlay permission resets on reboot

A known issue on some custom Android skins (MIUI, ColorOS). Re-grant the permission after each reboot or check your device's autostart settings.

Another app holds the mock location slot

Only one app can be the active mock location provider. Make sure no other mock location app is selected in Developer Options.
