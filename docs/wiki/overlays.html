Floating joystick

The floating joystick is a circular control that stays on top of whatever app you're using. Drag it to move your spoofed location in real time, even while a different app is open on screen. It keeps running on its own, and you can show or hide it from the floating widget without turning off spoofing.

  • Drag the center dial in any direction to move at your current speed setting (Walk, Run, or Bike).
  • Let go to stop moving. Your position stays at that last point until you drag again.
  • Drag the outer ring to reposition the joystick anywhere on screen.
  • Turning on lock mode keeps the joystick moving in the same direction after you let go, so the device keeps traveling in a straight line without you touching the screen. Turn lock on or off from the floating widget's lock button or from the joystick itself. While locked, tap the center dial to unlock it.
  • The joystick moves at whichever speed setting is currently active. Switch settings using the floating widget's speed cycle button without lifting your finger off the joystick.

Floating widget

The floating widget is a small panel with quick-access controls. Tap the round button to expand it, then use whichever controls you've set up in Settings, all without opening the app itself. It stays on screen even when you switch between other apps.

  • Tap the button again to collapse the panel back down to a single button when you don't need it, and tap once more to expand it again.
  • Drag the button to any edge of the screen to keep it out of your way.
  • Which controls appear, and in what order, is set in Settings → Menus → App Features. Changes take effect the next time you open the widget.
  • The widget always reflects what's currently happening, so if a route is playing, its pause and stop controls show up live right inside the widget.

Available widget controls

ControlWhat it does
Map shortcut Opens a compact map that floats above other apps, filling the screen. Lets you walk to a point, teleport, or start roaming without switching back to the main app.
Show / hide joystick Turns the floating joystick on or off. You need to start the joystick from the main app first: this button only shows or hides it, it doesn't start it from scratch.
Lock joystick Holds the current joystick direction after you release your finger, so your position keeps advancing. Useful for walking in a straight line hands-free. Tap again to unlock.
Routes picker Lists your saved routes. Tap one to start it, loop it, reverse it, or have it return to the start. While a route is running, it expands to show pause and stop controls.
Favorites picker Lists your saved favorites with one-tap teleport and walk shortcuts. You can also save your current spoofed position as a new favorite right from here.
Speed cycle Cycles through the Slow Walk, Walk, Run, Bike, and Drive speed settings with a single tap. The icon changes to show which one is active, and the new speed applies right away to the joystick, walk-to, and roaming.

Requirements

Both overlays need the Display over other apps permission turned on. The setup steps you see when you first open the app walk you through granting it. On some phone brands, like Xiaomi or Oppo devices, this permission can reset itself after a restart. If an overlay disappears, check your phone's autostart or battery settings.
