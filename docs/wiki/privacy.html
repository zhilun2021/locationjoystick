Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-31

App Data

locationjoystick stores all user data — routes, favorites, speed profiles, and settings — locally on your device using Room and DataStore. No app data is transmitted to any server.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID (resettable device identifier)
  • Device model, OS version, and language
  • Approximate location (country/region, derived from IP address)
  • App interaction data (ad impressions and clicks)

This data is collected by Google and governed by the Google Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalised ads at any time via Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID on your device.

When required by applicable law (e.g. GDPR in the EU/EEA), the app will display a consent form before showing personalised ads.

Location Permission

ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION is used solely to centre the map on your current position and to record routes. Location data is never uploaded or shared.

Camera Permission

CAMERA is used solely for scanning QR codes during device-to-device data transfer. No images are stored or transmitted.

Contact

Questions or requests: open an issue at github.com/shortcuts/locationjoystick/issues.
