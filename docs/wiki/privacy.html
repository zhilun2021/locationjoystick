Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-31

App Data

locationjoystick stores all user data — routes, favorites, speed profiles, and settings — locally on your device using Room and DataStore. No app data is transmitted to any server.

Location Permission

ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION is used solely to centre the map on your current position and to record routes. Location data is never uploaded or shared.

Camera Permission

CAMERA is used solely for scanning QR codes during device-to-device data transfer. No images are stored or transmitted.

Contact

Questions or requests: open an issue at github.com/shortcuts/locationjoystick/issues.
