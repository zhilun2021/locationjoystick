Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-31

App data

locationjoystick keeps all your data (routes, favorites, speed profiles, and settings) on your device. None of it is sent to any server.

Location permission

Location access is only used to center the map on where you are and to record routes. Your location is never uploaded or shared with anyone.

Camera permission

Camera access is only used to scan QR codes when transferring data between devices. No photos or video are stored or sent anywhere.

Screen reading for compass tracking

The app includes an optional feature called "Compass tracking" (Settings → Menus → Tap to Walk), turned off by default. When you turn it on, the app takes a screenshot of your screen right before you tap a location, so it can read the on-screen compass direction of the app you're using. This lets a tap turn into the correct location update even when the map on your screen isn't pointing north. The screenshot is read on your device to find the compass direction and is never saved or sent anywhere. This feature isn't a tool for people with disabilities. It stays off unless you turn it on yourself.
