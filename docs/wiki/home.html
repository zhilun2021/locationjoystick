Home screen

The main hub after onboarding. Navigate to any feature from here.

  • Tap any card to navigate to Map, Routes, Favorites, or Settings.
  • Open the navigation drawer (hamburger icon) to jump between screens from anywhere in the app.
  • The status bar shows whether mock location is currently active.

Last remembered location

When you reopen the app, locationjoystick restores the last spoofed position automatically. No need to re-enter coordinates after a restart. Toggle this in Settings if you prefer to always start fresh.

Background service

Once spoofing is started, the app keeps running in the background while you use other apps or the screen is off. A persistent notification shows that spoofing is active — tap "Stop" in the notification to end it without opening the app.

If Android stops the app in the background (common on devices with aggressive battery management), spoofing stops and your location reverts to real GPS. Re-open the app to restart.

Routes, roaming, and walk-to all continue uninterrupted while the screen is off or another app is in the foreground.
